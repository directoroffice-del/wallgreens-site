Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Who We Are

Wallgreens FormSolutions LLP ("Wallgreens", "we", "us", "our") is a limited liability partnership registered in India, with its principal place of business at Mumbai 400007, Maharashtra. We supply formwork, scaffolding, and engineered timber products to the Indian construction industry.

For privacy enquiries, contact us at: mumbai@wallgreens.in

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Contact enquiry data: Name, company name, phone number, email address, and message content submitted via our website contact form or WhatsApp.
  • Quote request data: Project details, product requirements, delivery address, and timeline shared when requesting a quotation.
  • Communication records: Records of correspondence via email, phone, or WhatsApp related to orders and service delivery.

We do not collect payment card information through this website. All financial transactions are handled via direct bank transfer or formal invoicing.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotations
  • To process and fulfil product orders and service engagements
  • To communicate order status, dispatch notifications, and delivery updates
  • To maintain records required for GST compliance and statutory accounting
  • To improve our website and services based on user interaction patterns

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information on the following bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To fulfil orders and service agreements you enter into with us
  • Legitimate interests: To respond to pre-sales enquiries and manage our business relationships
  • Legal obligation: To maintain records required under Indian tax and company law

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy. Customer and transaction records are retained for a minimum of 7 years to comply with GST and accounting obligations. Enquiry data from contacts who do not become customers is deleted within 24 months.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mumbai@wallgreens.in.

7. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics to understand visitor behaviour (e.g., page views, referral sources). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. If we implement analytics tools, they will be disclosed here and consent obtained where required.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: Font files are loaded from Google servers — Google may log the request. See Google's privacy policy for details.
  • WhatsApp (Meta): If you contact us via the WhatsApp button, your interaction is subject to Meta's privacy policy.
  • Google Maps: Our contact page embeds a Google Map — Google may collect data as per their privacy policy.
  • EmailJS: Our contact form uses EmailJS to deliver messages to our email inbox. Message content is processed by EmailJS servers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
