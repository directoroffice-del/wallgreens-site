South Asia's largest construction equipment fair

EXCON 2025 — the 13th edition of the CII-organised construction equipment exhibition — ran from 9 to 13 December 2025 at the Bangalore International Exhibition Centre (BIEC). The five-day event drew an estimated 1.2 lakh business visitors and 1,250 exhibitors from 20 countries across 3.5 million square feet of display area, cementing EXCON's position as the third-largest construction equipment expo in the world.

Wallgreens FormSolutions LLP participated as an exhibitor for our second consecutive major trade fair, bringing an expanded product line-up and live assembly demos to a significantly larger stall.

What we showcased

Our stall featured live, full-scale assemblies of four core product lines:

  • Mäch Deck — our modular aluminium slab formwork, assembled live on stand so visitors could see the panel, prop and drop-head system working as it does on a real slab pour.
  • Aluminium Scaffolding — our lightweight mobile-tower system, displayed at working height to demonstrate the stability, lock mechanism and load-bearing capacity.
  • J70 StiMBERTM Basic, PLUS & PRO — the full three-grade range of H20-profile timber beams — Basic for cost-sensitive slab work, PLUS for standard-cycle projects, and the heavy-duty PRO for bridge decks, metro viaducts and marine formwork.
  • Stall experience zone — brochures, material samples and technical literature covering our full product catalogue, with engineers on hand to discuss project-specific requirements.

Moments from the show

Across five days we hosted visitors from across India's construction and infrastructure sectors — main contractors, PMC teams, PSU engineers, and a strong showing of international delegations from the exhibition's seven international pavilions.

By the numbers

  • ~1.2 lakh (120,000) business visitors across five days
  • 1,250+ exhibitors from 20 countries, including 134 international companies
  • 3.5 million sq ft of exhibition space — the largest edition of EXCON to date
  • 7 international pavilions — France, Germany, Italy, South Korea, Turkey, China and the UK
  • 300+ product launches and live equipment demonstrations
