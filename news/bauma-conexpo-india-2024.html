A record-breaking edition

bauma CONEXPO INDIA 2024 — the country's largest international trade fair for construction machinery, building material machines, mining machines and construction vehicles — was held from 11 to 14 December 2024 at the India Expo Centre in Greater Noida. The four-day event drew more than 51,000 trade visitors from 83 countries and featured 984 exhibitors across 135,000 square metres of hall and outdoor space, making it the largest edition in the fair's history.

Wallgreens FormSolutions LLP participated as an exhibitor, showcasing a focused selection of formwork, scaffolding and engineered timber systems designed for Indian construction conditions.

What we showcased

Our stall gave visitors a hands-on look at four of our most-requested product lines:

  • Mäch Deck — our modular aluminium slab-formwork system for fast deck cycles in high-rise and commercial construction.
  • Circular Wooden Formwork — engineered plywood shuttering shaped for columns, silos and architectural curves, with reusable steel reinforcement for repeat pours.
  • J70 StiMBERTM Basic & PRO — our H20-profile timber beams, shown in the Basic duty-cycle grade and the heavy-duty PRO variant with reinforced chord timbers for bridge, metro and high-load slab work.
  • Shuttering Plywood — film-faced birch and hardwood shuttering boards engineered for clean off-form finishes and multi-cycle reuse.

Moments from the stall

Across the four show days we met contractors, project engineers and procurement heads from across India and abroad — discussing live project requirements, sampling our beams and decking panels, and running technical demos on site.

By the numbers

  • 51,118 trade visitors from 83 countries
  • 984 exhibitors, of which 25% international
  • 135,000 sq m of indoor and outdoor exhibition space
  • 6,298 productive buyer-seller meetings
  • 20,000+ products and solutions unveiled on show floor

Thank you to everyone who stopped by our booth — it was a privilege to demonstrate the next generation of Wallgreens formwork to the industry.
