Cup Lock Scaffolding

Versatile Steel Scaffolding · Medium-Duty Construction & Access

Cup Lock is India's most widely used scaffolding system. Wallgreens supplies high-quality galvanised Cup Lock components with consistent dimensions, verified load ratings, and complete range availability — backed by pan-India logistics for fast project deployment.

Key Features

  • Widest component availability in India — interchangeable with market-standard fittings
  • Lower cost per m² vs Ring Lock — ideal for residential and mid-rise projects
  • Simple cup-and-blade mechanism — minimal training required for assembly
  • Full range available: base jacks, u-heads, ledgers, cross-braces, and toe boards
  • Available in galvanised or painted finish to suit project specification
  • Pan-India stock at multiple Wallgreens depots for fast mobilisation
  • Compatible with all standard planks, ledgers, and jacks from Indian manufacturers
