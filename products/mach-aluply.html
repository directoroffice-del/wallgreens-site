Wall & Column Formwork

Mach AluPly

Lightweight aluminium column and wall formwork — only 2 components, crane-free operation, 200 sqm on Day 1 with just 4 labourers.

  • Only 2 components: Panel and clamps — the simplest column and wall system available, enabling near-foolproof installation with minimal training
  • Crane-free operation: Lightweight aluminium panels are handled manually by 2–3 workers — eliminates crane dependency and keeps costs down
  • Up to 4 cycles per month: Early concrete stripping capability enables faster floor-to-floor cycle times than conventional timber or steel panel systems
  • 60 kN panel load capacity: High-strength panels handle significant concrete pressure without horizontal bracing — site stays clean and open
  • Birch plywood sheeting: Superior surface quality delivers clean, consistent concrete faces — reduces remedial plastering costs
  • Zero material waste: Modular, plug-and-play design — panels combine for any column or wall geometry without cutting
  • 50 sqm per labourer per day: Proven productivity rate from live projects — significantly outperforms conventional systems
  • Up to 20-year service life: Aluminium frame is corrosion-free and requires no painting — low lifetime maintenance cost
🏢

High-Rise Shear Walls

Ideal for repetitive shear wall and core wall pours in residential and commercial towers — the lightweight frame enables rapid floor-to-floor stripping and re-erection.

🏛️

Columns

Square and rectangular columns up to 900×900 mm formed using standard panels and external corners — no custom fabrication required for standard column grids.

🚇

Basement Retaining Walls

The 60 kN/m² pressure rating handles deep basement wall pours. Works alongside Ring Lock falsework for combined wall-and-slab basement pours.

🏗️

Lift Shafts & Stairwells

Internal corners and filler panels allow Mach AluPly to form tight internal geometries such as lift shafts and stairwells with minimal make-up pieces.
