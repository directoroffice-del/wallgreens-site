Mach Deck

Modular Slab Formwork System · Wallgreens Proprietary

Mach Deck is Wallgreens' high-performance modular panel shoring system for slab construction — engineered for fast installation, early stripping, and superior productivity. With only 3 basic components and a spring-based drop-head mechanism, it delivers 268 sqm on Day 1 with just 6 labourers. Trusted by contractors on landmark projects including Tata Semiconductor (Guwahati), Airport City (Bangalore), and Yashoda Hospital (Hyderabad).

ParameterValue

Productivity268 sqm / Day 1 with 6 labourers (44.6 sqm/labourer/day)
  • Only 3 components: Panel, drop-head, and duty prop — the simplest modular system available, reducing training time and assembly errors
  • Spring drop-head: Quiet, precise spring-release mechanism allows early striking of panels while props remain in place — no shock loads on the slab
  • Zero X-bracing: High-strength props eliminate the need for tripods or horizontal bracing — site remains open and unobstructed for other trades
  • Birch plywood deck: Superior surface durability compared to standard film-faced plywood — delivers consistent, high-quality concrete soffit finish
  • Panels reusable in 3–4 days: Fast stripping and reuse cycle reduces the total panel inventory needed on site
  • 44.6 sqm per labourer per day: Proven productivity metric from live project data — significantly outperforms conventional timber shuttering
  • Lightweight aluminium: Crane-free panel handling — reduces equipment costs and accelerates erection on tight floor plates
  • Compatible with standard concrete mixes including high-strength and self-compacting grades
