Film Faced Plywood

Film Faced Densified Shuttering Plywood · IS 4990 Compliant

Wallgreens Film Faced Densified Shuttering Plywood is engineered for heavy construction formwork — hardwood eucalyptus layers bonded with 100% phenolic (waterproof) adhesive and protected by a 120 GSM red phenol film coating. Built to resist the moisture, pressure, and repeated use demands of concrete shuttering.

Key Features

  • High density eucalyptus core: Hardwood construction provides superior durability and resistance to impact damage under repeated formwork use
  • 100% phenolic adhesive: Fully waterproof bonding — panel integrity is maintained through multiple wet-concrete cycles without delamination
  • 120 GSM red phenol film: Protective face coating delivers smooth, releasable concrete surface and resists moisture absorption
  • Excellent tensile strength: Resists pulling and stretching forces during stripping — panels remain flat after multiple reuse cycles
  • Strong bending resistance: Eucalyptus hardwood core prevents cracking or breaking under concrete pressure loads
  • Wide thickness range: From 12 mm to 40 mm — suitable for all slab, wall, column, and beam formwork applications
  • Custom dimensions available: Panel lengths up to 3200 mm and widths up to 1675 mm — or cut to size on request
  • IS 4990 compliant — meets Indian Standard for plywood for concrete shuttering
