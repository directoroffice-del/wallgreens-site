J70 StiMBERTM

Hybrid Steel-Timber Formwork Beam · Retaining Walls, Beams, Columns & Slabs

The J70 StiMBERTM is a unique hybrid formwork beam — combining the tensile strength of YST 310 prime steel with a timber filler core. This combination delivers the rigidity of steel with the on-site workability of timber: it is nailable through the timber filler, reduces dependency on raw timber, and achieves up to 10 reuse cycles per unit. Available in three variants to match your project requirements.

Key Features

  • Hybrid construction: Steel channel binds the timber filler under load — combining the workability of wood with the structural reliability of steel
  • Hot-dip galvanised: 70-micron HDG coating resists corrosion on coastal and waterlogged sites — same grade as structural scaffolding
  • Nailable on site: Plywood sheathing can be fixed by nailing directly into the timber filler — familiar workflow for site carpenters, no special tools needed
  • Reduces raw timber use: Significantly lower timber volume compared to sawn timber — supports sustainable site practices
  • Up to 10 reuse cycles: Lower cost per use compared to traditional sawn timber which typically achieves 3–5 cycles
  • Steel end caps: HDG 2 mm steel end caps protect beam ends from moisture and impact damage
  • 20-year service life: The galvanised steel frame outlasts the timber filler — fillers can be inspected and replaced as needed
