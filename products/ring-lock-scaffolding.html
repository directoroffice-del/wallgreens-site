Ring Lock Scaffolding

High-Load Modular Scaffolding · Heavy Infrastructure & Bridge Work

Ring Lock Scaffolding is a high-capacity modular system for heavy construction applications. The 8-point rosette connector allows multi-directional ledger connections without special fittings — enabling complex scaffolding geometries required for bridge falsework, metro viaducts, and heavy industrial structures.

Key Features

  • 8-point rosette connector allows multi-directional connections — no special fitting required
  • Hot-dip galvanised finish provides superior corrosion protection for coastal and waterlogged environments
  • Handles extraordinary loads for bridge deck falsework and heavy transfer slabs
  • Flexible bay widths from 0.75 m to 3.0 m adapt to varied structural geometries
  • Fast erection and dismantling — purpose-designed for repetitive infrastructure applications
  • Full engineering support — load calculations, falsework drawings, and inspection checklists provided
  • Stocked at multiple Wallgreens depots pan-India for rapid mobilisation

Typical Applications

🌉

Bridge Falsework

Supports bridge deck and girder pours where high vertical loads and complex geometry demand Ring Lock capacity.

🚇

Metro & Railway

Elevated viaduct slabs, station roofs, and platform covers requiring scaffolding up to 20m working height.

🏭

Industrial Structures

Heavy concrete structures, plant foundations, and industrial building frames with large slab loads.

🏗️

High-Rise Transfers

Transfer plate slabs and podium decks in high-rise construction where loads exceed cup-lock capacity.
