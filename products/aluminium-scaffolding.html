Aluminium Scaffolding

Mobile Aluminium Tower Scaffolding · Indoor & Outdoor Access

Wallgreens Aluminium Mobile Scaffolding towers are engineered for interior finishing, MEP installation, and low-to-medium height access work. Weighing 70% less than equivalent steel scaffolding, they can be assembled, moved, and reconfigured by just 2 workers — dramatically reducing downtime between work fronts.

Key Features

  • Tool-free assembly: Erect and dismantle without any tools — frames click and lock together for fast repositioning between work fronts
  • Alloy 6063/6082 T6: High-strength aviation-grade aluminium — lightweight yet capable of 200 kg/m² platform loading
  • 2–14 m working height: Full height range in one system — from low-level finishing work up to 14 m free working height
  • 150 kg safe working load per platform: Accommodates workers, tools, and materials simultaneously
  • Rust-free: Aluminium construction eliminates corrosion — no contamination risk on sensitive concrete, MEP, and finishing surfaces
  • Lockable castors: Swivel castors with built-in brakes — safe working position with easy repositioning when required
  • Ideal for HVAC, electrical, false ceiling, painting, cladding, and all finishing trades
  • Suitable for both indoor and outdoor use — EN 1004 compliant for internal and external applications
