J50 StiMBERTM

Hybrid Steel-Timber Formwork Beam · Lightweight Shuttering Support

✓ Available on Rental · Pan-India

The J50 StiMBERTM merges the strength and durability of YST 310 steel with a timber filler core in a compact 50mm × 50mm cross-section. Weighing just 3 kg per metre, it is the lightest beam in the StiMBERTM range — easy to handle without equipment, nailable on site, and designed to achieve up to 10 reuse cycles. Available in Basic and Plus variants. Offered on rental or outright purchase — an efficient way to scale inventory up or down across project phases without tying up capital. Key Features

  • Hybrid construction: Steel channel and timber filler work together — structural steel backbone with the on-site workability of wood
  • Ultra-lightweight at 3 kg/m: The lightest beam in the StiMBERTM range — manual handling without crane or equipment at all common lengths
  • Hot-dip galvanised: 70-micron HDG coating provides long-term corrosion protection for outdoor and wet-site conditions
  • Nailable: Plywood sheathing can be fixed by nailing directly into the timber filler — familiar workflow for site carpenters
  • Reduces raw timber consumption: Hybrid design uses significantly less timber than conventional sawn timber shuttering
  • Up to 10 reuse cycles: Multiple reuse cycles deliver lower cost per pour versus sawn timber
  • Steel end caps: HDG 2 mm end caps protect beam ends from moisture ingress and mechanical damage on site
  • Rental & outright purchase: Rent for the pour cycle or buy outright — flexible commercial terms, with Wallgreens managing return logistics. See rental economics →
