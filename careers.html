Sales Engineer — Formwork Systems

Full-Time Mumbai / Pan-India 2–5 Years Experience
Apply Now

We are looking for a result-oriented Sales Engineer with knowledge of formwork systems to develop and manage client relationships with contractors, developers, and EPC companies across India.

Key Responsibilities
  • Identify and develop new business opportunities in the construction sector
  • Present and demonstrate Wallgreens formwork solutions to site engineers and project managers
  • Prepare technical proposals, quotations, and order management
  • Achieve monthly and quarterly sales targets
Requirements
  • B.E./B.Tech in Civil Engineering (preferred)
  • 2–5 years of experience in construction material sales or formwork
  • Strong communication and negotiation skills
  • Willingness to travel to project sites across India

Site Engineer — Formwork & Scaffolding

Full-Time Project Sites (Pan-India) 1–3 Years Experience
Apply Now

Join our field engineering team as a Site Engineer responsible for supervising formwork and scaffolding installation, ensuring safety compliance, and providing on-site technical support to contractors.

Key Responsibilities
  • Supervise Mach Deck, StiMBERTM, and scaffolding installations at project sites
  • Conduct safety checks and ensure compliance with IS standards
  • Train contractor workforce on correct system usage
  • Coordinate with the operations team for material dispatch and returns
Requirements
  • Diploma or B.E. in Civil Engineering
  • 1–3 years of site experience in formwork or construction
  • Good understanding of safety protocols (IS 14687)
  • Comfortable with extensive site travel

Logistics & Operations Coordinator

Full-Time Mumbai HQ 2–4 Years Experience
Apply Now

Manage the end-to-end logistics of formwork equipment — from warehouse dispatch to site delivery, tracking, returns, and refurbishment scheduling.

Key Responsibilities
  • Plan and coordinate dispatches of formwork material to project sites across India
  • Maintain inventory records and track equipment utilisation
  • Coordinate with transport vendors for timely deliveries
  • Manage documentation for dispatch, returns, and damage reports
Requirements
  • Graduate with 2–4 years in logistics / supply chain (construction preferred)
  • Proficiency in Excel and ERP/inventory software
  • Strong organisational and communication skills
  • Mumbai-based, fluent in English and Hindi

Technical Support Engineer

Full-Time Mumbai / Remote 3–6 Years Experience
Apply Now

Provide pre-sales and post-sales technical support — including formwork design, load calculations, shop drawings, and on-site troubleshooting for our contractor clients.

Key Responsibilities
  • Prepare formwork layout plans and load calculation reports for clients
  • Support sales team with technical inputs during proposal stage
  • Conduct product training workshops for contractor engineers
  • Handle technical queries and provide remote troubleshooting support
Requirements
  • B.E./M.E. in Civil / Structural Engineering
  • 3–6 years experience in temporary works or formwork design
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD; knowledge of STAAD/safe loads a plus
  • Excellent written and verbal communication
