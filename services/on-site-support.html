On-Site Engineering Support

Dedicated Wallgreens engineers on your project — supervising installation, ensuring safety, and maximising cycle performance.

Our On-Site Role

Your Engineering Partner on the Ground

A Wallgreens site engineer is assigned to your project from the first pour. They work alongside your site team — not above it — to ensure the formwork system performs exactly as designed.

This isn't just supervision. Our engineers actively troubleshoot, adapt layouts to field conditions, and prevent problems before they cause delays.

Site Engineer Responsibilities

  • Supervise first installation cycle — ensure correct assembly per design drawings
  • Train site workers and supervisors on system assembly and stripping
  • Conduct pre-pour safety inspection and sign-off
  • Monitor prop load distribution and stripping sequence compliance
  • Identify and resolve field deviations from design layout
  • Document pour records, cycle times, and equipment utilisation
  • 24/7 availability for urgent site queries
