Technical Consultation

Formwork design, load calculations, layout plans, and shop drawings — before your project breaks ground.

What We Provide

Engineering Support From Day One

Before a single prop is erected, Wallgreens' engineering team studies your structural drawings, concrete programme, and site constraints to develop the optimal formwork solution.

Our technical consultation service includes everything a project team needs to execute formwork safely and efficiently — from load calculations that satisfy your structural engineer, to layout plans that guide your site supervisor.

Deliverables

  • Formwork layout plans for each typical floor / zone
  • Prop load calculations and bearing pressure checks
  • StiMBERTM beam span tables and safe working load charts
  • Stripping sequence and re-propping methodology
  • Method statement for temporary works
  • Material list / schedule of quantities
  • Shop drawings for non-standard configurations
  • Site safety checklist aligned with IS 14687
