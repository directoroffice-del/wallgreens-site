StiMBERTM Hybrid Steel-Timber Beams Explained

How Wallgreens' unique hybrid beam technology outperforms sawn timber for formwork — and how to choose between J70 and J50 variants for your project.

What Is a Hybrid Steel-Timber Beam?

StiMBERTM is a unique concept that combines the structural strength of steel with the workability of timber in a single formwork beam. A YST 310 prime steel channel forms the backbone of the beam, with a timber filler core bonded inside. HDG steel end caps protect both ends.

This hybrid construction gives StiMBERTM a structural advantage over conventional sawn timber — the steel channel provides consistent, predictable load performance, while the timber filler allows plywood to be nailed directly into the beam exactly like a standard timber member. No special tools, no factory modifications needed. Beams are supplied in pre-cut standard lengths — site cutting is not required.

The result: the reliability of engineered steel with the simplicity of timber — at a cost and weight that makes it practical on every type of formwork project.

How StiMBERTM Beams Are Constructed

  1. Steel channel forming — YST 310 prime coil steel is roll-formed into the structural channel profile
  2. Hot-dip galvanising — The steel channel is hot-dip galvanised to 70 microns for corrosion protection
  3. Timber filler selection — Filler material is selected by variant: European Spruce (Basic), OSB (Plus), or Waterproof Plywood with LVL base (Pro)
  4. Filler insertion — The timber filler is precision-fitted into the steel channel
  5. HDG end cap fitting — 2 mm HDG steel end caps are fitted to both ends to protect against moisture and mechanical damage
  6. Length cutting — Finished beams are cut to standard lengths: 1.5 m · 1.8 m · 2.4 m · 3.0 m · 3.6 m · 12 m

Choosing Between J70 and J50 StiMBERTM

J70 StiMBERTM (70mm × 50mm cross-section, 4.5 kg/m) is the heavier beam in the range, with 3 variants — Basic, Plus, and Pro. Specify J70 for:

  • Higher load-bearing requirements — walls, columns, beams, and heavy slabs
  • Longer span conditions requiring greater stiffness
  • Wet or aggressive site environments where the Pro variant's waterproof plywood filler adds durability
  • Projects where reuse frequency justifies the Pro or Plus upgrade

J50 StiMBERTM (50mm × 50mm, 3 kg/m) is the lightweight option with 2 variants — Basic and Plus. Specify J50 for:

  • Standard shuttering applications where J70 capacity is not required
  • High-volume projects where minimising beam weight reduces labour and handling costs
  • Cost-sensitive residential and commercial projects seeking the benefits of hybrid steel-timber at economy price
  • Situations where manual handling without any equipment is essential
