Who We Are

India's Most Trusted Formwork Partner

Wallgreens FormSolutions LLP is a construction technology company headquartered in Mumbai, supplying engineered formwork systems, scaffolding, and hybrid steel-timber beams to India's leading contractors and developers. Trusted by L&T, Tata Projects Ltd., and KEC International Ltd. — partners who set the standard for quality in Indian infrastructure.

Founded in 1965 by Tarachand M. Chowatia, our journey spans six decades of continuous evolution — from bearing trading in Ahmedabad, to non-ferrous metals in Mumbai, to becoming a pioneer in formwork technology with a pan-India and international reach. Wallgreens FormSolutions was formally established in 2022 under the leadership of Siddhant Chowatia, bringing renewed focus on innovation and next-generation construction systems.

Our product portfolio — Mach Deck, Mach AluPly, StiMBERTM, and Ring Lock Scaffolding — is engineered to deliver faster cycles, safer operations, and superior build quality on every project.

Our Journey

Six Decades of Milestones

1965

Founded in Ahmedabad

Tarachand M. Chowatia establishes the company in Ahmedabad as a bearing trading enterprise — the foundation of a business that would evolve into India's construction technology leader.

1991

Expansion into Non-Ferrous Metals — Mumbai

Operations expand to Mumbai with entry into non-ferrous metals, establishing our presence in India's commercial capital and the roots of our formwork supply business.

2001

Entry into Plywood — Relocation to Bangalore

Wallgreens enters the plywood sector and relocates to Bangalore, positioning for rapid growth in the South Indian construction market.

2005

Largest Market Share for Shuttering in South India

Wallgreens achieves the largest market share for shuttering materials in South India — a milestone reflecting the trust of contractors across Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, and Kerala.

2009

Direct Birch Plywood Imports from Russia

Wallgreens begins direct birch plywood imports from Russia, ensuring consistent quality and competitive pricing for high-performance shuttering plywood across India.

2017

StiMBERTM Launched

Introduction of StiMBERTM — Wallgreens' proprietary hybrid steel-timber formwork beam. A unique concept combining YST 310 steel with timber filler, engineered to outperform conventional sawn timber shuttering.

2022

Wallgreens FormSolutions Established

Wallgreens FormSolutions LLP is formally established under the leadership of Siddhant Chowatia, bringing a fresh focus on innovation, technology-led construction systems, and pan-India scale.

2024

J70 StiMBERTM Evolves into Complete Formwork System

The J70 StiMBERTM expands from a standalone beam product into a complete integrated formwork system — with complementary components, accessories, and engineering support for full slab solutions.

2025

Mach Deck Partnership with Technocraft Industries

Wallgreens partners with Technocraft Industries to expand the Mach Deck supply chain — bringing greater capacity, faster dispatch, and wider pan-India availability for India's fastest-growing slab formwork system.

Today

Reimagining Construction

Preferred partner to L&T, Tata Projects Ltd., and KEC International Ltd. — Wallgreens continues to reimagine construction with intelligent formwork systems, combining steel, timber, and technology to shape the skylines of tomorrow.
